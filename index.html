Free Prompts

Start your reflection today.

A taste of the Art Is You journal. These prompts are yours, free. The full 60-prompt collection is inside the bundle.

💭
Identity
"Who would I be if I stopped shrinking for other people's comfort?"
Write for 10 minutes without stopping. Don't edit. Don't judge.
💸
Wealth
"What is one limiting belief about money I inherited — and is it actually true?"
Trace it back. Where did you first hear it? Who said it?
🌸
Beauty
"Describe the version of yourself that feels most beautiful. What does she do differently?"
Not what she looks like. What she does, thinks, prioritises.
✨
Manifestation
"Write a letter from your future self — from 3 years ahead."
Be specific. What did she figure out? What does she want you to know now?
🏛️
Old Money Energy
"What would I stop tolerating if I truly believed I was worth more?"
List it. People, habits, situations, thoughts. All of it.
